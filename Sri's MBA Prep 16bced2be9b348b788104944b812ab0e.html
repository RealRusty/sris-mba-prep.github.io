Exam Pattern

  • CAT has 3 sections
    1. Quantitative Aptitude (QA) = basic Math + some basic Physics
    1. Verbal Ability and Reading Comprehension (VARC) = basic English
    1. Logical Reasoning and Data Interpretations (LRDI) = basic Aptitude
  • total number of questions = 100 in 3 hrs (180 min)
    till 2015 to 2019 there were 100 questions but in 2020 due to pandemic there were 76 questions. so the case of 2020 can be considered as an exception. now what we need to look for is what happens in 2021 and 2022
  • Each section has almost equal weightage = 1/3 part = 33.3 % of total paper
    • QA = 34 qsns (marks = 102)
    • VARC = 34 qsns (marks = 102)
    • LRDI = 32 qsns (marks = 96)
